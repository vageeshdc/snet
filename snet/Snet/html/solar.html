Introduction to Solar Photovoltaics

SNet IIT Madras conducted its first workshop for the year, "Introduction to Solar Photovoltaics" on 28th January, 2012. The workshop, conducted in association with Central Electronics Centre (CEC) saw participation from an enthusiastic group of 44 undergraduate, graduate and doctoral students from all departments.
The workshop started with an 1 hr. introductory lecture by Prof. Jeevandoss. Conducted in both the morning and afternoon sessions, the blazing sun could not stop the participants as they progressed to the terrace to experiment with the solar panels.
The afternoon session ended at 4pm as the sun started going down. On the whole, it was an informative experience for all.
