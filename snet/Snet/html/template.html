Activities

Sustainability is a life style to be put in to practice rather than a topic to be preached. SNET focuses on effectively diverting human resource to learn and implement sustainable development practices in their life. With this aim, SNET opens up opportunities of learning by organizing various activities in areas of energy, water, waste, environment and nutrition. Optimization of electric power usage has been treated as a special category, owing to its nature of deeper impact and quick result. In addition, SNET is involved in a lot of studies and research on problems in implementing sustainable development in different areas and suggesting solutions to these hurdles. Outcomes of these studies are also used in increasing awareness among student community in IITM campus to take up environment protection and sustenance activities. Various promotional competitions are also organized to draw attention and provide platforms for student community to involve in making IIT Madras a model campus in the area of Sustainable lifestyle.

    • SNET is an organization characterized by professionalism with a human heart for environment. Its activities are organized mainly under three categories each with each serving its own intended purpose.
    Sustainable development activities
    Sustainability Development Activities, SDA involves in identifying various problems and causes that affect environment and ecological balance. SDA initiates studies and research to identify problems and performs extensive brain storming and discussion sessions to generate ideas on solutions to these issues. The ideas and methods that come up from it are put to feasibility analysis on ease of implementing, cost, payback time and environmental impacts. Primary objective of all these exercises is to come up with solutions for common man to follow and showcase it to the nation and world at large.
    SDA identifies with specific projects that can be taken up by SNET to implement inside or outside the campus.Â  Some of the interesting ideas that this group has proposes includes LED lights for corridors in hostels, Solid waste segregation techniques, Rain water harvesting, Flood prevention through ground water recharging (improving water percolation) etc.
    These ideas are then taken forward by Sustainable development initiatives group towards implementation.
    Sustainable development Initiatives management
    The initiative team operates in two ways-

    Sustainability awareness activities
    Awareness is major step in achieving sustainability. This team works in reaching out to public with facts on realities of environmental impact, climate change and influence of individual level activities on this global phenomenon. The team conducts direct awareness campaigns through Extra mural lectures, Workshops, Training sessions, informative posters, electronic media etc.
    The team also mobilizes the student community towards this common cause through incentivized exercises in the form of various competitions. The Sustainability Challenge is a techsoc competition which has become highly popular in the campus, involving various hostels competing among themselves to come up with a detailed study identifying problems in their hostels on sustainability and suggest solutions which are implementable in their respective hostels.
    As part of their global awareness and motivation program the awareness team along with the entire SNET community of the campus plans to organize a Global Sustainability Conference every year which will serve as a common practice to share ideas and to show case problems, solutions, research activities, products and achievements in the area of sustainability.
