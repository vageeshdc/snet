Sustainable Development Initiatives Management

The initiative team operates in two ways-

1. Implementing suggestions from SDA in Pilot scale to test the impact.
2. Implementing suggestion from SDA on a global scale, in case of techniques identified as having proven track record.
3. Helping other campuses to implement the techniques tested and proved feasible and effective by SNET.
4. Developing various products, gadgets, systems etc suggested by SDA to aid in sustainable development activities.

Activites:

  • 1. Techsoc sustainability Challange aimed to promote the studdent community to come up with fesable solutions for a certian environmental challange
  • 2. Introduction to Solar Photovoltaics workshop aimed to present insights on this clean energy.
