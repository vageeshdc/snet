Welcome


Living on this Earth, with all the differences and diversity, we all share a common feeling, though expressed in different ways. It is the deep sense of gratitude towards the Mother Earth for providing us with all our needs. But, we often fail to pay back this debt we owe her.

Although not intentionally, we all are directly or indirectly responsible for various kinds of destructive practices on earth. Our ever rising needs and unknowingly exercised greed has contributed to a lot of damage to our beloved mother earth.


We are a group of environment conscious volunteers engaged in practicing and promoting environment friendly and sustainable living practices, to create a model sustainable campus at IIT Madras for the nation to follow. Our group includes primarily students, supported by administration, Staff, alumni and various others in campus community.

Sustainability Network is all yours. Involve, share, learn and imbibe the Sustainable way of living. The art of caring and sustainable living - The IIT Way.

We are dedicated to bring awareness on conscious use of resources, decreasing waste and pollution, mobilizing people towards collective action on environment protection, promoting organic food and making sustainable practices a life style.

“We aspire to be a model campus which takes ‘0’ input from outside and gives ‘0’ output out of its boundary chiefly in terms of energy, water and fuel.
